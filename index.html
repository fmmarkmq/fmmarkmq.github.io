Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Rochester (UR), where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Chenliang Xu. Prior to that, I received my M.S.E degree in Financial Mathematics at Johns Hopkins University (JHU). and my B.Sc. degree in physics at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

I am broadly interested in:
- Trustworthy AI: hallucination, bias, privacy, and explainability;
- Forward Learning: likelihood ratio method, zeroth-order optimization;
- MLLM: Audio-visual LLM, Video LLM;

I am actively looking for collaboration on research. If you share similar interests, please feel free to contact me!
